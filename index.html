My Reflection

What I understand.

Philosophical Perspective (Reflection)

Out of all the topics, this one is what I like most. I feel like there is so much to learn, to analyze, and to understand. I like the way these philosophers think.

Physical Self (Reflection)

This topic made me understand simple things about my physical self that I did not know about. How a face shape, blood type, body type, etc., explains so much about yourself.

Material Self (Reflection)

It helped me realize the power of influence. How easy it is to influence someone in the things they buy. How, in reality, the things we buy the most are things we don't need.

Digital Self (Reflection)

That it is okay to be a FOMO or JOMO type of person. Because in reality, what matters is that we enjoy what we do and we are not hurting anybody.

Mental Self (Reflection)

We are all smart in our own ways. And that we all have our ways on how we process information and that's totally normal.

My Application

How will I apply it.

Through these insights, I've gotten a better awareness of myself: physically, intellectually, and digitally, and the factors that shape my identity. Embracing many points of view has improved my critical thinking and empathy, and understanding the importance of mindful choices has helped.  Accepting that it's okay to be different sometimes. Moreover , I plan to apply these concepts by becoming more self-aware, deliberate, and real in my growth and interactions with the people around me.
